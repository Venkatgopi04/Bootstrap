offset classes in Bootstrap:
=================
Bootstrap supports offset classes.The main objective of offset classes in bootstrap is meant
for alignment purpose.

==========================================
Device_Name                                                      offset_classes:
==========================================
For extral small device                                         offset-xs* 
For small device                                                   offset-sm*
For medium devices                                             offset-md* 
For large devices                                                  offset-lg*
===========================================
